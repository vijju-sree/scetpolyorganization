Project Work:

Students will work on a project in the final semester, which will help them apply the concepts learned throughout the program. The project can be in any area of ECE, such as robotics, communication systems, embedded systems, or VLSI design.

Industrial Training/Internship:

Students will undergo industrial training or internship in the final semester, which will provide them with hands-on experience in the industry. The training will be in a reputed organization, and students will work on a project or assist in the development of a product or system.
