Electrical And Electronic Engineering(EEE).

The design, development, and use of electrical systems, electronic devices, and electrical machinery are the focus of the engineering specialty known as Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE). It is an important component in the advancement of contemporary technology and is a combination of electrical and electronics engineering. the production, distribution, transmission, and use of electricity. The design and development of circuits, systems, and devices that use electronics, such as embedded systems, microcontrollers, and microprocessors. Electrical machines include transformers, generators, and motors. They are designed, built, and operated. Automation, robotics, and process control are all included in the design and implementation of control systems. computer networks, data transmission, and telecommunication are examples of communication systems. The study of electromagnetic waves, electromagnetic theory, and applications is known as electromagnetics. The organization, creation, and management of power systems, encompassing the production, distribution, and transmission of energy. Power Generation and Distribution: Power plants, transmission lines, and distribution systems are all designed and run using EEE. Electronics and Communication: Computer networks, communication systems, and electronic devices are all designed and developed using EEE. Industrial Automation: Robotics, automation, and control system design and implementation all involve the application of EEE. Transportation: The design and development of automotive electrical systems, including those for hybrid and electric cars, uses EEE.

Anti Ragging

Swarnandhra College of Engineering and Technology (SCET)
in Andhra Pradesh, India, like many institutions,has a
comprehensive anti-ragging policy to ensure a safe and
respectful environment for all students.Here’s an overview
of what such a policy. The policy defines ragging as any act
that causes physical or psychological harm to students. This
can include harassment, humiliation, or any form of bullying.
SCET enforces a strict no-ragging policy, which means that any
form of ragging is not tolerated. The institution maintains a
zero-tolerance stance to ensure that students are protected.
Preventive Measures Awareness Programs. The college conducts
awareness campaigns and orientations for students and staff
about the ill effects of ragging and the importance of
maintaining a safe environment. Rules and Regulations Clear
rules are established and communicated to students at the
beginning of each academic year, outlining what constitutes
ragging and the consequences of engaging in such behavior.
